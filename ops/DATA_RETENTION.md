# Data Retention And Privacy

## Data Inventory

- Lead data: business name, source, category, contact route, status, review notes.
- Trial data: owner reply reference, menu source references, confirmation state, trial URL, trial outcome.
- Paid customer data: menu source, confirmed menu facts, publish history, support record.

## Retention Rules

- Rejected or skipped leads may be kept for duplicate prevention, but must not be send-ready.
- Opt-out and do-not-contact markers are retained so future sends remain blocked.
- Declined trial pages are archived after manual review.
- Raw owner menu sources should be deleted or archived once the final owner-confirmed page and support record are complete.
- Generated public pages must not include internal lead IDs, source URLs, or review notes.

## Privacy Standard

Restaurant information received after reply is used only to create, review, publish, operate, or support the QR menu.
