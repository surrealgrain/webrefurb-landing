# Production Ready Means

For this project, production ready means the product can be sold, trialed, published, and supported for real restaurants without inventing process during execution.

## Required State

- Public site and live demos pass static and live health checks.
- The interactive demo keeps the current coral/white look and Show Staff List behavior.
- First-contact outreach is QR-first, reply-only, manually approved, and limited to email/contact forms.
- Trial state is tracked from request through accepted, build, owner review, live trial, converted, declined, or archived.
- Prices, descriptions, ingredients, and allergy notes publish only after owner confirmation.
- Archived or declined trial pages are not linked from public indexes.
- Lead records explain quality blockers in internal language only.
- Data retention, opt-out, sender identity, and failure runbooks are documented.
- The release gate passes before pushing.

## Manual-Only Items

- Native iPhone/Android device testing.
- Native Japanese credibility review.
- SPF/DKIM/DMARC changes in DNS.
- Real outreach batches and pilot sends.
