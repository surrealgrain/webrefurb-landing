# Business Policies

## Paid Conversion

- Product: English QR Menu + Show Staff List.
- Setup price: 65,000 yen tax excluded, 71,500 yen including 10% consumption tax.
- Payment method: bank transfer by default until another method is explicitly added.
- Work after a free trial continues only if the owner chooses to keep it.

## Updates

- One pre-launch revision is included.
- Post-launch updates are quoted separately unless manually agreed.
- Price, description, ingredient, and allergy changes require owner confirmation.

## Printing

- Default: owner prints the QR sign from the print-ready file.
- If the owner has no printer, handle manually for now. Do not advertise a paid print add-on until fulfillment is defined.

## Multi-Location

- One setup covers one restaurant/location menu.
- Multiple locations require manual review because prices, availability, hours, and staff flow may differ.

## Common Answers

- This does not replace the current menu.
- No app is needed.
- No new equipment is needed for the guest-facing QR page.
- Menu changes after launch are possible after owner confirmation.
- Allergy notes are shown only after owner confirmation.
- The QR sign is provided as a print-ready file.
