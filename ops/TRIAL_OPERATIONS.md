# Trial Operations

## Trial States

- `requested`: owner asked about or requested a trial.
- `accepted`: owner agreed to try it.
- `intake_needed`: waiting for current menu source or confirmation details.
- `build_started`: operator is building the QR menu.
- `owner_review`: waiting for owner confirmation.
- `live_trial`: trial page is live.
- `trial_ending`: day 7 follow-up is due.
- `converted`: owner is keeping the product.
- `declined`: owner declined after trial.
- `archived`: public page has been taken out of active use.

## Follow-Up Timing

- Day 5: ask whether anything needs adjustment before the trial ends.
- Day 7: ask whether they want to keep it.
- Day 10: if there is no decision, ask once more and then archive manually if needed.

## Follow-Up Copy Principles

- No pressure.
- No implication that payment is required unless they choose to continue.
- No menu facts are changed without owner confirmation.
- No public page remains active after a decline unless explicitly approved.
