How to Order

Choose your skewer, then select Salt 塩 or Tare Sauce たれ. Point to the item and hold up fingers for quantity.

Salt vs Tare

Salt 塩 brings out natural flavour. Tare たれ is a sweet soy glaze grilled on. Both work with any skewer.

Drink Pairing

Draft beer or highball pairs well with salt skewers. Sake suits tare and liver skewers.
