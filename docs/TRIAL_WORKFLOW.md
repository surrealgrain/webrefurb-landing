# Trial Workflow

## Scope

Active product: `english_qr_menu_65k` / English QR Menu / 65,000 yen tax excluded.

Trial offer: free 1-week trial. The owner continues only if the QR menu helps guests and staff. If the owner declines after the trial, no cost is charged and the trial page is retired.

## First Contact

- QR-first outreach only.
- Link the generic demo: `https://webrefurb.com/demo/`.
- Ask for a reply only.
- Do not ask for menu photos in first contact.
- Do not claim a lead-specific sample was made from the restaurant's menu.
- Test sends only go to `chris@webrefurb.com`; real sends require explicit manual approval.

## Trial Setup

1. Confirm restaurant name, category, and email/contact-form route.
2. Create the trial QR menu page.
3. Generate QR code and print-ready A4 QR sign.
4. Publish prices, descriptions, ingredients, and allergy notes only after owner confirmation.
5. Send the trial link and QR sign by email.

## During Trial

- Owner prints one A4 QR sign and places it near the counter or table.
- If printing is inconvenient, coordinate the next step manually.
- Restaurant information is used only to create and operate the QR menu.

## Trial Outcome

- If owner continues: issue the 65,000 yen tax-excluded setup quote, confirm payment path, then keep the published menu live.
- If owner declines: retire the trial page and record the outcome as declined/no charge.
- If owner needs changes: quote updates separately unless manually agreed.

## Policy Gaps Now Made Explicit

- No monthly fee by default.
- Post-launch updates are available on request and quoted separately.
- Multiple locations require manual scope review; do not assume one QR menu covers every location.
- Print-and-mail QR signs are not included by default.
